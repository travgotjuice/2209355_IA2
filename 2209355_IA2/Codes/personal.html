Personality and Impact on Academic Life

My strong tendency toward independence is one distinguishing feature of my personality. I'm naturally independent and have a propensity to take care of problems and obstacles on my own rather than actively seeking out social contacts.Although having a strong sense of independence might be useful in some circumstances, it has presented difficulties for me, notably in my academic career because I am now seeking a college degree. This tendency for self-sufficiency has had a somewhat negative effect on my college experience. Academic success requires a level of socializing and cooperation that is required of college students.
